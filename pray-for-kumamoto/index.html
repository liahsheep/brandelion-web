#PrayForKumamoto

We are a group of Instagrammers who concern about the recent earthquake occurred in Kumamoto, Japan.

Those who have paid a visit to the sakura nation must have agreed that this country is not only famous for her beautiful scenery and unique architecture, but is also appreciated for her humanities and cultural heritage. She gives us lots of photographic inspirations, and defines beauty in many ways. It is unbearable to see this earthquake has taken away many homes and loved ones from people living in that region. We hope to do something about it. So we put on our thinking cap and come up with an idea: why don’t we make Japan’s beautiful scenery a motivation for restoration? This is how this exhibition which consists work of European, American and Asian Instagrammers, come into place.

Most of us are not professional photographers, and this, is exactly why our style is more diversified. We believe this would allow you to see Japan at different angles. Please kindly support our charity sale if you like our work. The sale proceeds, after deducting the cost of this event, will be donated to Kumamoto Prefecture.

#PrayForKumamoto Exhibition

Date: 17–30 June 2016
Venue: Youth Square, Chai Wan

You can also support Kumamoto at our online charity sale even you cannot attend the exhibition!
